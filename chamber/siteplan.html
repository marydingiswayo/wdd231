FreshBites Kitchen

Site Name

FreshBites Kitchen - I chose this name because it clearly communicates that the website is about fresh, homemade food. "FreshBites" suggests quality ingredients and delicious small meals, while "Kitchen" emphasizes the homemade aspect.

Site Purpose

The FreshBites Kitchen website serves two primary purposes:

  • To provide users with easy-to-follow recipes for homemade meals using fresh ingredients
  • To offer cooking tips and techniques for beginners and experienced cooks alike
  • To create a community platform where users can share their cooking experiences and recipe modifications

Target Audience Scenarios

Scenario 1: Beginner Cook

"How can I find simple recipes that don't require advanced cooking skills or special equipment?"

User Need: Easy recipes with step-by-step instructions and common ingredients.

Scenario 2: Health-Conscious User

"Where can I find nutritional information and healthy alternatives for traditional recipes?"

User Need: Filter recipes by dietary restrictions (gluten-free, vegan, low-carb) and view nutritional facts.

Scenario 3: Meal Planner

"Can I save recipes and create weekly meal plans with shopping lists?"

User Need: Recipe saving functionality and meal planning tools with integrated shopping lists.

Color Schema

Primary Color

#2E8B57 (Sea Green)

Usage: Main headers, navigation bar, primary buttons, and accents

Secondary Color

#FF8C00 (Dark Orange)

Usage: Call-to-action buttons, highlights, and hover effects

Accent Color

#F5F5DC (Beige)

Usage: Background sections, card backgrounds, and content areas

Text Color

#333333 (Dark Gray)

Usage: Main body text, paragraphs, and general content

Typography

Primary Font: Poppins

Poppins Light (300) - This is used for body text and general content.

Poppins Regular (400) - This is the default font for paragraphs.

Poppins SemiBold (600) - Used for subheadings and important text.

Poppins Bold (700) - Reserved for main headings and page titles.

Secondary Font: Roboto

Roboto Light (300) - Used for captions, meta information, and footnotes.

Roboto Regular (400) - Alternative for long-form content and blog posts.

Typography Application:

  • Main Headers (H1): Poppins Bold, 2.5rem, Sea Green (#2E8B57)
  • Sub Headers (H2-H3): Poppins SemiBold, 1.8rem-2rem, Dark Gray (#333)
  • Body Text: Poppins Regular, 1rem, Dark Gray (#333)
  • Navigation & Buttons: Poppins SemiBold, 1rem, White on Sea Green
